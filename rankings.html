How We Calculate Rankings for 5-8 Team Tournaments

Each player recieves individual rankings to account for singles and doubles tournaments.
A win in the first round (or wild card round) will net you 10 points.
A win in the semifinals will net you 20 points.
A win in the 3rd place game will net you 30 points.
A win in the finals will net you 40 points.
