Rules

All official foosball matches will have the following components, unless otherwise specified:

  • Games are played on the taller, more sacred table.
  • Games are played with one and only one ball.
  • Games must be won by 2 points or more. "Win By 2" Rule is in effect.
  • The winner of the match is declared after one side scores 10 goals.
  • Players switch sides after every 5 goals scored.
  • Goals scored with the goalie or the final defender count as 2 points each.
  • Goals with any other player are considered 1 point each.
  • If the ball is unreachable by either side, the team that calls "foos" first has possession.
  • The foosball must be thrown in without any part of the hand within the table.
  • The playing field may not recieve any external wind from blowing or other mechanisms.
  • Moderate spinning is allowed (constant spinning not tolerated).
